FROM SKIES TO SCREENS

My Journey into Web Development

Roots and Horizons

I grew up in beautiful Bavaria, near Lake Chiemsee, where I developed a love for nature and hospitality. I pursued my education in Hotel- and Tourism Management at the School of Young Tourism in Freilassing, completing my training in 2013. For almost 10 years now, I've called Ismaning, a charming suburb of Munich, my home. Here, I enjoy the peaceful, more rural surroundings while still being close to the vibrant city life of Munich.

From Trolleys to Code

Since 2014, I’ve been a flight attendant, moving trolleys up and down the aisles. Now, I’m moving boxes - but this time, it’s all about code instead of snacks! My passion for web technologies led me to pursue a degree in Media Informatics, which I’m currently working on. While it might seem unconventional, both fields require quick thinking, problem-solving, and creating smooth experiences - whether in the air or on the web.

Exploring New Dimensions in Web Design

I’m excited to expand my skills in web design and development and I'm looking forward to exploring the creative and technical aspects of the field in more depth. This new journey is something I’m really enthusiastic about, and I’m open to collaborating with others who share a passion for innovation and problem-solving.

Nature, Music & Diving

Besides work and studying, I love spending time in nature, playing the electric bass, and going scuba diving in my free time. These activities help me recharge and stay balanced as I continue my journey in both my personal and professional life.
